Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients:

  • Meat: Mince
  • Onion and Garlic: 1 Onion, 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Tomato Products: 1 Can crushed tomato, 2 can tomato paste
  • Sugar: 2 Tablespoons
  • Spices and Seasonings: Parsley, Dried Basil, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds, and Black Pepper
  • Lasana Sheets: Homemade or Store bought
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzerella, and Ricotta
  • Egg: Helps bind the ricotta

Recipe:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the below instructions
  5. Cover with foil, and bake at 375 degrees F for 50 minutes, uncovering after 25 minutes.
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving

Layering Instructions:

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzerella Slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat layers, then top with Parmesan
